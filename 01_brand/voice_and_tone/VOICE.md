# Voice and Tone

## The test

Before shipping any copy, ask: **does this sound like a flight instructor or a marketing team?**

Flight instructor wins.

## Voice principles

**Direct.** Short sentences. Lead with the point. Cut throat-clearing.

**Honest.** We tell hard truths because we respect the trader. We never soften data into mush.

**Calm.** Trading is stressful enough. The platform is a steady presence, not another stressor.

**Respectful.** Our audience knows what a limit order is. We never over-explain. We never condescend.

**Considered.** Every word earns its place. If it can be cut, cut it.

## Tone matrix

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing headline | Confident, spare | "Most journals log what happened. This one tells you why." |
| Onboarding | Functional, warm | "Which prop firm? We'll tune your drawdown tracking." |
| Empty state | Gently inviting | "No trades yet. Import a CSV to get started — 60 seconds." |
| Trade imported | Quiet confirmation | "Imported 47 trades from Tradovate." |
| Positive insight | Factual, not cheerleading | "Your first 5 trades of the day are +EV on every day this month." |
| Hard insight | Factual, not scolding | "Off-playbook trades: –$2,140 this month. Playbook trades: +$1,890." |
| Cooldown nudge | Calm, data-led | "You've taken 3 trades in the last 5 minutes. Your stats on 3+ trade clusters: –$180 average. Pause for 5?" |
| Drawdown warning | Urgent, not alarming | "$240 from daily loss limit. 2 contracts at full stop would breach." |
| Error | Straightforward, never apologetic theater | "Couldn't parse that file. Expected Tradovate format — check the export settings." |
| Email subject | Plain, specific | "Your week in review" — not "🚀 Your EPIC trading week is here!" |

## Copy before / after

**BEFORE (generic SaaS):**
> Welcome to [Brand]! 🎉 We're SO excited to have you on board. Let's start your trading journey together!

**AFTER:**
> Let's set up your account. Three questions, then you can import your first trades.

---

**BEFORE (fear-based):**
> ⚠️ WARNING! You are approaching your DAILY LOSS LIMIT! Stop trading NOW to protect your account!

**AFTER:**
> $240 from daily loss limit. A 2-contract NQ stop-out from here would breach.

---

**BEFORE (cheerleading):**
> 🔥 INCREDIBLE WORK! You CRUSHED it today! +$1,240! Keep up the amazing momentum! 💪

**AFTER:**
> +$1,240 today. 73% win rate, 1.4R average. Your best session type this month: NY open, ES.

---

**BEFORE (moralizing):**
> We noticed you've been overtrading lately. Remember, discipline is key to success! Take a deep breath and stick to your plan.

**AFTER:**
> 14 trades today. Your passing combines averaged 6. Trades 7–14 this month: –$820 net.

## Words and phrases

**Use these:**

setup · playbook · session · edge · process · discipline · signal · noise · drawdown · basis · tape · execution · bias · thesis · reckon

**Avoid these:**

journey · excited · amazing · incredible · crush · crushing it · beast mode · ninja · guru · rockstar · alpha (as slang) · literally / absolutely (as intensifiers) · game-changer · next-level · 10x · moonshot · lambo · hustle · grind (as praise) · let's go · LFG

**Emoji policy:**

Sparingly. Never in-app except for very specific state indicators. In marketing, one well-placed emoji per piece maximum, and never the rocket, fire, money-bag, or chart-up. If it belongs in a sports betting ad, it doesn't belong with us.

## The rewrite test

When in doubt, rewrite any sentence to be 30% shorter. Then ask if it still says what needed to be said. Usually yes. Ship the shorter one.
